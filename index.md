## Background

I completed my **PhD at the University of South Australia** in 2017, researching automated network configuration generation. My thesis, *Abstractions and Transformations for Automated Data Network Configuration*, presented a compiler-based approach to transform high-level network specifications into device configurations across multiple platforms and protocols.

The work produced **AutoNetkit**, an open-source tool written in Python that's been used in Cisco's VIRL project and by network engineers in industry. I presented this work at PyCon AU 2013 ([recording on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EGK5jjyUBCQ)).
